Quohog

Quohog is a serif typeface, designed with classical characteristics.

Let's get together

To effectively study the characteristics of a serif typeface, I explored in detail one of my favourite type classes—the slab serif. I’ve always been fascinated with the history, and though the slab has been very effectively modernized—it still very much reminds me of the old Snake Oil salesman signs from the 1800s.

I chose to do a slab serif font, but with classical characteristics. I included classical traits like the bowl of a lower case g, or the tail of an upper case Q. I also had the ascenders on the lower case letters extend past the cap height. I wanted to do all this while maintaining an equal weight and geometric appearance.

Quohog is a modern, geometric, slab serif font built with inspiration from classical roman characters.
