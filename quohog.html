Quohog

Quohog is a serif typeface designed with classical characteristics.

Let's get together

To effectively study the characteristics of a serif typeface I explored my favourite, the slab serif. Iâ€™ve always been fascinated with the history, and though the slab has been very effectively modernized, it still very much reminds me of the old Snake Oil salesman signs from the 1800s.

I chose to do a slab serif font, but with classical characteristics. I included classical traits like the bowl of a lower case g, or the tail of an upper case Q.. I wanted to do all this while maintaining an equal weight and a geometric appearance.

Quohog is a modern, geometric, slab serif font built with inspiration from classical roman characters.

The ultimate specimen

Algonquin College has recently added a 3D printer to to the print shop on campus. This allowed me to take my study of serif typefaces to another level.

I chose to print the lower case g because this character best displays the marriage of classical and slab serif families.
