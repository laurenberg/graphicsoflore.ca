Office Atomicus

Using parallax effects in Adobe After Effects, I revisit a classic piece by Salvador Dali.

An old favourite

I’ve always found Dali’s work mesmerizing, my absolute favourite piece is his photograph Dali Atomicus. I always wondered how he managed to execute this photograph limited by the technology in 1948.

I still don't know how he managed to get the cats and water to photograph so clearly.

A day in the life

This is a look into my home office, I live in a small apartment in downtown Ottawa with my boyfriend, and our two cats. As any cat owner knows, cats have no sense of personal space.

This scene represents how much goes on in my small office, and how little privacy and space I get to myself.

Meet the stars

My photographer and I practiced several times plopping pillows onto the couch before the cats took the stage. We got the shot on the first try for each of them, no kitties were harmed in the making of this video!

Both Fen and Moose were compensated with catnip and tripe for a job well done.
