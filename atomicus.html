An old favourite

I’ve always found Dali’s work mesmerizing, and my absolute favourite one of his pieces is one from his photography collection Dali Atomicus. I always wondered how he managed to execute this photograph with the technology in 1948.

I still don't know how he managed to get the cats and water to photograph so clearly.

A day in the life

This is a look into my home office, I live in a small apartment in downtown Ottawa with my boyfriend Tyler, and our two cats. As any cat owner would know, cat’s have no sense of personal space.

Unlike Dali, I have access to some pretty cool technology which brought this concept to life.

Meet the stars

Tyler and I practiced several times plopping pillows onto the couch before the cats took the stage. We got the shot on the first try for each of them, and both kitties were compensated with catnip and tripe for a job well done.

No kitties were harmed in the making of this video!
