Impact Report

An anual showcase of the Ontario Centre of Excellence's achievements, and their projections for the next fiscal year.

Addressing a sensitive topic

The Ontario Centre of Excellence focuses in youth mental health services, and every year provides it’s contributors with an Impact Report.

Youth mental health is extremely important, but it can be a difficult issue to talk about. The Ontario Centre of Excellence needed a light-hearted approach to otherwise very sensitive subject matter.

In order to make this content easier to digest I had to find a way to present the subject matter in a more approachable way, making it easier to talk about.

Organizing a lot of good news

The Ontario Centre of Excellence has a lot to proud of, and their 2016–2017 year had much to boast. This required a comprehensive layout of both the print and web versions of the Impact Report.

The printed version was just a summary of the full Impact Report available online, so the real challenge of structure came into the navigation of the website. In two of the sections, a sub-navigation was used to divide larger content into more palatable portions.

Making a cohesive plan for how to organize the content was the most important way to make sure readers absorbed as much information as possible.
