Impact Report

The anual showcase of the Ontario Centre of Excellence's achievements, and their projections for the next fiscal year.

Addressing a sensitive topic

The Ontario Centre of Excellence focuses in youth mental health services, and each year provides contributors with its Impact Report.

Youth mental health is extremely important, but it can be a difficult issue to talk about. The Ontario Centre of Excellence needed a welcoming appearance to address a sensitive topic.

In order to make this content easier to digest I focused on community, making connections, and making a positive impact.

Organizing a lot of good news

The Ontario Centre of Excellence has a lot to proud of, their report was extensive. This required a comprehensive layout of both the print and web versions of the Impact Report.

The printed version was a summary of the full Impact Report available online, so the real challenge was the structure of the website navigation. A custom sub-navigation was used to divide larger content into more palatable portions.

Making a cohesive plan to organize the content was critical in making sure readers absorbed as much information as possible.
