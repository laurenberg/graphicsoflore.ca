Packaging Redesign

Castor & Pollux has the only organic pet food in the country, though their packaging doesn't reflect the same values.

Unique selling point

Castor & Pollux has a special organic formula just for cats, ORGANIX. Their clients trust them to use all natural ingredients, so I found it unfitting to sell their product in unnatural packaging.

When analyzing other products there are several cat food companies that package in paper, so why not Castor & Pollux?

Since this is a specialty cat food unique from others on the market, I wanted the product to look like something pet-parents would instantly associate with quality.

Think like a human

Cats are carnivores. Sometimes there's added roughage for vitamins and minerals, but they should primarily only be fed meat.

ORGANIX doesn't only offer meat though, they offer certified all organic ingredients. This isn't your discount grocery store soggy, smelly meat section.

Where do you go when you want premium meat?
The butcher.
